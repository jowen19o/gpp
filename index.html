Submission Format
  • - The Problem: A description of the dimensions of the issue its scope and why it exists.
  • - Your Proposed Solution: How you suggest we begin to solve the issues--your vision.
  • - Research: Other models and their success rates.
  • - Optimization: How you will test and scale your proposed solution?
  • - What do you need from us?
Evaluation Criteria:
  • All entries are evaluated by a team of experts according to the following four criteria:
  • Understanding of the problem
  • Originality of the solution
  • Quality of research and exposition
  • Viability and commitment to the solution

Areas of Need

Small Businesses

Small business are taking huge hit. Among the 'non-essential' businesses being shut down, are family-run restaurants, movie theaters, gyms, concert venues, and many construction sites

Unemployment

As more and more 'non-essential' businesses get shut down, unemployment only continues to climb. The UN is predicting as many as 200 million jobs will disappear by June.

Health Care

At least half of the world's population is too poor to access essential health care services. In a global pandemic, this is a huge problem. Health care expenses are already pushing hundreds of millions of people into poverty and this will only get worse as more patients need access to testing.

Education

Schools worldwide are shutting down, with shelter in place orders sweeping across nations. Students are being throw into a completely different learning environment. How will families adapt?

Broken Families

Families around the world are under attack. In America, one in four families doesn’t have a father in the home. With families isolated together at home, what opportunities are there to finally bring healing to the hurt?
